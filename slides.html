What is NVivo?

Software developed to support Qualitative Data Analysis

  • Available for Mac and PC
  • Free for UBC students, faculty, and staff
  • Supported by the UBC Library Research Commons
If purchasing separate license, costs more than $1000 USD. So if you're at all curious about the software now is the time to try it. The Research Commons offers

Why use NVivo?

New ways to access and interact with your sources

Document your research and thought process

Keep citations, sources, and notes organized

Integration with citation managers

  • EndNote
  • Mendeley
  • RefWorks
  • Zotero
